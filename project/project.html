Smart Street Light Project

An IoT-based system to control and monitor street lights for energy efficiency. The system uses LDR sensors to detect ambient light and automatically turns lights on or off. It also reports faulty lights to a central dashboard.

IoT Arduino Energy Saving

Smart Microgrid Simulation

Designed and simulated a microgrid for a remote community using MATLAB/Simulink. The project integrated solar panels, a battery storage system, and a diesel generator, with a controller to manage load balancing and optimize power flow.

MATLAB Simulink Renewable Energy

Factory Automation with SCADA

Developed a SCADA system for a small-scale manufacturing plant model. The system monitored conveyor belt speed, motor temperature, and production count, leading to a simulated 20% increase in efficiency.

SCADA PLC Industrial Automation

AI-Powered Battery Optimization

Utilized machine learning algorithms in Python to predict energy demand and optimize the charge/discharge cycle of a battery energy storage system (BESS). This project aimed to reduce electricity costs by leveraging time-of-use tariffs.
